Marking a Milestone in Tech Leadership: The CSSL GenZ Chapter Launch at Horizon Campus

In a momentous event that echoed the promise of technological advancement and leadership, the launch of the Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL) GenZ Chapter at Horizon Campus in Malabe, Colombo, took place on July 12th, 2023. The prestigious KCM auditorium was the backdrop for this significant occasion, commencing at 2:30 PM. The launch ceremony was graced by the distinguished presence of Dr. Ajantha Athukorala, a prominent figure in the tech industry, as well as esteemed CSSL authorities and members of Horizon Campus' top management. The auditorium buzzed with anticipation as tech enthusiasts and professionals gathered to witness the initiation of a new era in technology leadership.

One of the event's highlights was the exposing of the official website of the CSSL GenZ Chapter. The website is poised to become a hub for information, resources, and collaboration, providing a digital platform for the burgeoning community of tech enthusiasts associated with Horizon Campus. A pivotal moment during the ceremony was the introduction of the new committee members who will steer the CSSL GenZ Chapter to new heights. These individuals, chosen for their passion, expertise, and commitment to advancing technology, are expected to play a crucial role in shaping the chapter's initiatives and fostering a culture of innovation.

A particularly noteworthy aspect of the launch was the generosity extended to the aspiring students in attendance. In a gesture aimed at nurturing and supporting the next generation of tech leaders, free memberships to the CSSL GenZ Chapter were bestowed upon the eager and ambitious students at the event. This initiative not only opens doors for educational and networking opportunities but also reflects the chapter's commitment to inclusivity and accessibility in the tech community. Adding a collaborative dimension to the launch, members of the CSSL GenZ Chapter of the General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (KDU) actively participated in the ceremony. This cross-institutional collaboration not only underlines the significance of the event but also highlights the unity and shared vision of the CSSL GenZ community in advancing the field of technology collectively.As the CSSL GenZ Chapter at Horizon Campus takes its first steps, it sets the stage for a future where innovation, collaboration, and leadership will define the tech landscape. The launch ceremony not only marked the inception of a new chapter but also symbolized the commitment of Horizon Campus and CSSL to nurturing a generation of tech leaders who will shape the digital future of Sri Lanka. The CSSL GenZ Chapter at Horizon Campus is poised to become a catalyst for technological advancements, and the journey has just begun.
