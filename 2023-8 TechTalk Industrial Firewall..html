TechTalk Recap: Safeguarding Online Security in Today's Connected World

Recently, the Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL) orchestrated another triumph with "TechTalk," a captivating event held at the UCSC building on November 22nd, starting from 4:30 PM onwards. This gathering of tech enthusiasts delved into the imperative topic of "Dealing with Online Security Risks in Today's Connected World," recognizing the significance of staying informed and taking proactive measures in our modern, interlinked society. CSSL's commitment shone through, ensuring that "TechTalk" delivered on its promise to educate and empower attendees.
Dr. Malaka N. Halgamuwe, Senior Lecturer at the Centre for Cyber Security Research and Innovation, took center stage as the esteemed speaker. His expertise in cybersecurity illuminated the event, offering invaluable insights into the realm of online security. Dr. Halgamuwe's contribution added depth to the discussions, enriching the audience's understanding of the critical need for heightened online security measures.
The participation of the CSSLGENZ chapter from KDU highlighted the inclusive nature of CSSL's events. As a student chapter under the CSSL umbrella, KDU GENZ had the privilege to engage in this insightful event. CSSL's commitment to providing students with remarkable opportunities underscores the value it places on nurturing the future leaders of the tech industry.
Gratitude was extended to the annual partners of CSSL, acknowledging their invaluable support and contribution to the success of the event. Their ongoing commitment reflects a shared vision of fostering knowledge-sharing platforms within the tech community.
CSSL's impeccable organization and knack for curating compelling topics and distinguished speakers were evident yet again with "TechTalk." The event was a testament to CSSL's ability to bring together industry experts and thought-provoking themes, resulting in another successful chapter in their series of impactful initiatives.
