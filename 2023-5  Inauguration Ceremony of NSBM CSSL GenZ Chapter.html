Article on Inauguration Ceremony of NSBM CSSL GenZ Chapter

In a momentous occasion for the National School of Business Management (NSBM), the university recently unveiled its much-anticipated CSSL GenZ Chapter, led by the newly appointed President, Mr. Isiwara Kumarage, and his dedicated Executive Committee. The official inauguration ceremony of CSSL GenZ chapter held on July 19th, 2023, marked the establishment of a dynamic chapter within the computing faculty of NSBM. The event attracted students, industry professionals, and supporters, all eager to witness the birth of the CSSL GenZ chapter in NSBM Green University and gain insights into its vision for the future. The ceremony was a celebration of empowerment, signaling a commitment to nurturing the next generation of tech leaders within the CSSL community of NSBM.

On behalf of Computing Society of Sri Lanka, the President of CSSL Dr.Ajantha Athukorala and immediate past president Mr.Damith Hettihewa and the chapter patron CSSL GenZ chapter of KDU/project co-ordinater of CSSL GenZ chapters Mrs.Nirosha Wedasinghe graced the event, adding an excitement and anticipation in the air. Mr. Chandraprakash Ramesh, Vice-President of CSSL GenZ chapter of KDU representing on behalf of Mr.Induwara Rupasinghe, President of GenZ KDU chapter (2023-2024), welcomed the CSSL GenZ Chapter of NSBM Green University to the CSSL GenZ family and presented the remarkable projects and activities accomplished during the last two years under the leadership of Anupa Ranasinghe and Gagana Geethanjana. Together, they have set the bar high for the GenZ community.

Welcoming the CSSL GenZ chapter OF NSBM Green University to the CSSL family underscores the commitment to inclusivity and recognizes the diverse talents and perspectives that the new generation brings. It sets the stage for a dynamic exchange of ideas, knowledge transfer, and the cultivation of a vibrant tech community.
